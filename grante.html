End-to-End Process

  1. Consultation & Specification: define brief, budget, and timeline.
  2. Templating: digital laser capture ensures precision.
  3. CNC Fabrication: mitres, grooves, radius corners, cut-outs, sculpted edges.
  4. Installation & Sign-off: protect, fit, seam, seal, polish, and brief care.

Care & Maintenance

Quartz: mild soap and water; avoid harsh abrasives and direct high heat. Granite & Marble: seal on install and reseal periodically. See our care guide.
